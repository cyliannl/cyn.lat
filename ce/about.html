Gabriella Stid

Writer, journaler, and the person behind The Quiet Page. Gabriella didn't set out to teach anyone anything — she just kept a journal long enough that other people started asking how she did it.

Her Story

Healing me softly with my words.

That line started as a note to herself, scrawled in the margin of a journal on a particularly hard week. It became the closest thing Gabriella has to a mission statement.

For years, journaling was the one practice she came back to no matter what else changed — through hard seasons, quiet ones, and everything in between. Over time, the way she wrote settled into a rhythm: four steps she'd return to almost without noticing. Arrive. Reflect. Release. Return.

The Quiet Page is that rhythm, written down so anyone else can use it too. It isn't a method she invented to teach — it's the one she lived, shared because it helped, and might help you as well.
