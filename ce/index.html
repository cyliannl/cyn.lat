The Method

Four quiet steps, taught inside the book.

This isn't another productivity system. It's the same slow, honest practice Gabriella uses herself — laid out in The Quiet Page as four steps that meet you exactly where you are.

Arrive

A short, grounding ritual to settle the body before the pen touches paper.

Reflect

Guided prompts that gently surface what's actually asking for your attention.

Release

A page built for letting go — not fixing, not solving. Just releasing.
